    • Introduction

    Hidden in plain sight, a centuries-old conflict rages on. Supernatural monsters known as 'Curses' terrorize humanity from the shadows, and powerful individuals known as 'Jujutsu' sorcerers use mystical arts to exterminate them. When high school student Yuji Itadori discovers the withered finger of the legendary Curse of Sukuna Ryomen, he unexpectedly finds himself drawn into a bloody conflict. Attacked by a Curse drawn by the power of the finger, Yuuji makes a rash decision to defend himself, gaining the power to fight the Curses in the process, but also unwittingly releasing the malevolent Sukuna into the world once again.
